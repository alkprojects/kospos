# Civil Service Commission (CSC)

Owns Civil Service Rules (Vol I–IV) governing classification, exams, eligibility lists, appointments, probation.

## Resources

- [CSC department page](https://www.sf.gov/departments--civil-service-commission) — index of Commission policies, rules volumes, hearing procedures.
- [Civil Service Rules Vol I (most City employees)](https://www.sf.gov/resource--2022--rules-apply-most-city-employees-civil-service-commission-vol-i) — Rules 101–122 including classification, exams, eligible lists, appointments, probation.
- [Rule 109: Position Classification (Vol I)](https://www.sf.gov/reports--july-2024--rule-109-position-classification-civil-service-commission) — defines HR Director classification authority, status grants, appeals. Current as of July 2024.
- [Rule 209 — Position Classification (Vol II, Police uniformed)](https://www.sf.gov/reports--july-2024--rule-209-position-classification-and-related-rules-civil-service-commission)
- [Rule 309 — Position Classification (Vol III, Fire uniformed)](https://www.sf.gov/reports--july-2024--rule-309-position-classification-and-related-rules-civil-service-commission)
- [CSC Policy Adviser #8 — Selection from Eligible Lists](https://media.api.sf.gov/documents/CSC-Policy-Adviser-8-Selection-from-Civil-Service-Eligible-Lists_slGs3Ly.pdf) — example of CSC guidance docs; reissued on revision.
